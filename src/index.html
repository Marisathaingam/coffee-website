About Us

Welcome to Marisa Coffee in Maribor, Slovenia, the perfect spot for coffee enthusiasts and lively conversations! Step into a warm, cozy atmosphere where every cup is crafted with love and every visit feels like catching up with an old friend. Whether you're here to sip, chat, or simply unwind, we're all about creating memorable moments over exceptional coffee. Come for the coffee, stay for the vibes, and leave with a smile!
